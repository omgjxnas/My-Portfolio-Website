TaskMaster - planning app

TaskMaster

Planning app design

TaskMaster is a UI/UX design for a modern planning app that I created with a focus on intuitive task organization. The design allows users to easily create and categorize to-do lists, plan with a clear calendar view, and share lists with others, fostering team collaboration.

Clean and functional interface, TaskMaster delivers seamless and visually engaging task management. This concept combines aesthetics with effective usability to help users achieve their goals with greater clarity and motivation.

Farmers Hub website

Farmers Hub

Farmers product e-shop design and branding

Farmers Hub is an interactive web design that connects people with high-quality local produce from Czech farmers. This concept supports eco-friendly and bio standards, emphasizing sustainability and ethical farming practices. The design allows users to easily subscribe to and purchase fresh, sustainable, and "no-harm" products that respect nature.

Farmers Hub is designed to provide an intuitive and welcoming environment where natural quality and convenience meet the needs of modern customers.

FoodFly - Food delivery app

FoodFly

Food delivery app design

Foodly Delivery is my very first UI/UX design, created to offer a simple and clear app for fast food delivery at the best prices. This project allowed me to learn the basics of UI/UX design, giving me a better understanding of how to create an intuitive user interface and a functional structure for an app that provides clarity and ease of use for users.

The app is designed for users to browse restaurant offerings, quickly assemble orders, and track delivery in real-time. With a focus on user experience, I prioritized a clean, modern look that supports efficiency and provides a seamless food delivery experience.

Porsche - Full responsive website

Porsche website

Prototype of Porsche website with all media queries and full responsive

The Porsche Website is a UI/UX design project where I focused on translating a static design into a functional, responsive landing page. Created as a sleek, engaging introduction to the Porsche brand, this landing page showcases a full-screen layout with all media queries applied for seamless responsiveness across devices.

This project allowed me to learn the fundamentals of converting design into code, with an emphasis on maintaining aesthetic quality and functionality. Although currently without clickable elements, this prototype serves as a foundation for a fully interactive web experience.
